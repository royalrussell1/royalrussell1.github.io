Middle East

The middle east consists of mostly arab countries. Most of the countries speak arabic but many have their own language like Iran has Persian and Turkey has Turkish and there is some Hebrew as well.

The middle east has a lot of diversity in history, religioun, ethnicity and nationality. The middle east is like a mosaic of cultures while arabs form the majority the region also hosts berbers, kurds, Jews, Persians, Turks and other ethnic and religious minorities.

The middle east has seen the rise and fall of many Empires including the Ottoman Empire and Caliphates There were also trade routes like the Silk road and spice trade connecting the Middle east to Asia, Europe and Africa

South Asia

There are many ancient roots in South Asia. The religioun of Hinduism was rooted in south asia along with Buddhism.

They were also home to one of the first ever civilizations known as the Indus River Valley Civilization and it was also where the Indo Aryan Migration happened

East Asia consists of Countries such as China, Mongolia, Japan, Korea and Vietnam.
