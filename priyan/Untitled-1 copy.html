Building In Fortnite

Building is a central mechanic in the game of fortnite, as it allows you to block opposing players from shooting you.
Press, "Q" to enter the building interface where you can find multiple default builds that you can place in the game. You can place: "Cones", "Ramps", "Walls" and "floors"
Cones are important as, they disallow you to build on top of the are where they are placed. This is important as, this can stop players from building in front of you, blocking your shots
Ramps are important as they allow you to get to a higher position than your oponent. This allows you to shoot people more effectively as, its harder to shoot you if your higher than if you where lower.
Walls are important as they allow you to block the shots of your oponent. This allows you to take less damage will let you live for longer.
Floors are important as they allow you to get cover from ariel attacks. This allows you to take less damage which will let you win more fights.
