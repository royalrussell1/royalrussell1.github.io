The Controlls of Fortnite

Moving is one of the most important mechanics in fortnite as it allows you to traverse the area of the game.
Press, "W" to move forwrds, Press "A" to move to the left, Press, "D" to moove to the left and press, "D" to move backwards.
Press, "SHIFT" to start sprinting. This allows you to move faster. Press, "CTRL" to crouch. This allows you to be smaller which makes it harder for enemies to kill you.
Press, "Left Click" on your mouse to shoot your weapon. You can only do this when holding a weapon
