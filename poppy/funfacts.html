Here are some fun facts that you may not know about Dolphins:
  • Dolphins are very playful and caring animals.
  • Orcas are actually just a species of Dolphin.
  • There are up to 45 differing species of Dolphins.
  • Dolphins' lives can span from 40 all the way to 80 years old.
  • Dolphins can reach 20 feet above the water when jumping out of it.
