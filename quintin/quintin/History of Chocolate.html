The history of Chocolate.

The history of Chocolate. The history of Chocolate goes at least 4,000 years back. The cocao tree was first domesticated by the Mayo-Chinchipe culture. The Olmec civilization was the first to turn the cacao plant into pure chocolate. Chocolate was first introduced to Europe in the 16th century, the word chcoolate was traced by the Aztec word "xocatl.
