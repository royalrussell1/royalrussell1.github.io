Different pointe shoe brands

There are a lot of different pointe shoe brands, but the leading pointe shoe brands are: Bloch, Grishko, Capezio, Freed of London, Gaynor Minden and Suffolk Pointe shoes.

The type of pointe shoe you get can alter depending on your foot shape and size.

You can buy pointe shoes on a website, but there's no knowing if they'll fit your foot or not, so its best to get them fitted at a dance shop!!!
