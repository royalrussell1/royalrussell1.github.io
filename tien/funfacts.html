Here are some facts you probably didn't know about turtles:


  • Tortoises are actually a type of turtle!

  • Turtles can weigh over 1500 pounds, 700kg!

  • Turtles can hold their breath for up to 45 minutes!

  • Leatherback tutles can travel distances of 10,000 miles each year when they migrate!

  • Turtles have existed for over 200 million years!

  • Turtles live in every continent apart from Antarctica

  • Sea turtles lay up to 100 eggs at a time!
