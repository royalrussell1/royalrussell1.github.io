Introduction:

Turtles are a type of reptile found in North, South, and Central America, Africa, Australia, and Eurasia. Turtles tend to be found in warm, sub-tropical waters and can live for 10 to 150 years! They are known for their slow and gentle nature, and people from all over the world travel to swim with them in the ocean. Turtles have been alive for 200 million years, making them the oldest species of reptile! Also there are more than 350 species, including Pig-nosed turtles, Pond turtles, Leatherback turtles, Sea turtles & Loggerhead turtles. However, global warming and plastic pollution is becoming an increasing problem for these harmless animals. Unfortunately, they are now threatened by extinction and thousands are dying every year.
